Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 16, 2026

1. Introduction

ConnectBoy ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and gaming services (the "Service").

We prioritize transparency and user control, ensuring that your gaming experience remains secure. By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Registration: Username, email address, password, and optional display name
  • Referral Program: Referral codes and points earned through our player recruitment system
  • Payment Metadata: Transaction history, wallet balances, and reference tokens (actual payment processing is handled by secure third-party providers)
  • Communication: User-to-user invites, support tickets, and system notifications

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Tokens & Identifiers: Push notification tokens (FCM) and platform type (iOS/Android/Web)
  • Gameplay Dynamics: High scores, achievements, and game session data
  • Access Logs: IP address, browser type, and approximate location for regional compliance
  • Social Logins: Provider keys from third-party services like Google and Apple (if used for authentication)

3. AI and Machine Learning Usage

To provide a better gaming experience, we may use Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) technologies for:

  • Fair Play & Anti-Cheat: Detecting anomalous gameplay patterns to prevent unfair advantages
  • In-Game Matchmaking: Optimizing player matching based on skill level and latency
  • Content Optimization: Improving game balance and suggested features based on player trends

We do not use AI for significant automated decision-making without appropriate oversight.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: Maintaining your profile, wallet, and referral track record
  • Service Continuity: Managing real-time game sessions and synchronization
  • Security: Preventing fraud, unauthorized account access, and malicious behavior
  • Engagement: Sending push notifications for game invites and account updates
  • Compliance: Meeting regulatory requirements and responding to legal requests

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. Sharing only occurs in limited circumstances:

  • Third-Party Providers: Cloud hosting, push notification services, and payment gateways
  • Public Profiles: Usernames and rankings visible on global leaderboards
  • Legal Necessity: When required to comply with law or protect our rights and safety
  • Corporate Transactions: During mergers, acquisitions, or asset transfers

6. Your Privacy Rights & Control

We provide tools to manage your personal information:

  • Account Update: Change your username and profile details directly via settings
  • Account Deletion: Request the permanent removal of your account and all associated data
  • Data Access: Request a summary of the data we hold regarding your account
  • Revocation: Withdraw consent for non-essential data processing at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact our Privacy Team at connectboygames@gmail.com.

7. International Data Protection

We operate globally and may transfer data to jurisdictions with different protection laws. We implement standard safeguards to ensure your data remains protected regardless of its processing location.

8. Children's Privacy

Connect Boy does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we become aware of such data, we will take immediate steps to delete it. We recommend parental supervision for users under 18.

9. Data Security

We implement robust technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including password hashing (BCrypt), secure token-based authentication, and encryption during transit.
