Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 27, 2026

1. Introduction

Connect Boy ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy in an evolving digital landscape. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile applications and website (the "Service").

We prioritize transparency and user control, ensuring that your gaming experience remains secure and private. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Registration: Username, email address, date of birth, and password
  • Payment Information: Credit card details and billing address (processed via secure, industry-standard third-party providers)
  • Profile Information: Avatar, bio, display name, and optional profile data
  • Communication: Messages sent through in-game chat, support tickets, and inquiries
  • Feedback: Surveys, reviews, and community participation data

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Metadata: Device type, OS version, unique identifiers, and network information
  • Gameplay Dynamics: High scores, achievements, session duration, and feature interaction
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address for regional compliance and matchmaking
  • Analytics: Performance metrics, crash reports, and engagement patterns

3. AI and Machine Learning Usage

To provide a better gaming experience, we may use Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) technologies for:

  • Fair Play & Anti-Cheat: Detecting anomalous gameplay patterns to prevent unfair advantages
  • In-Game Matchmaking: Optimizing player matching based on skill level and latency
  • Personalized Content: Suggesting games or features that align with your interests
  • Customer Support: Using automated systems to categorize and respond to common inquiries more efficiently

We do not use AI to make automated decisions that have legal effects on our users without human oversight.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Reliability: Maintaining, and improving our global gaming infrastructure
  • Multiplayer Synergy: Enabling seamless real-time interactions and social features
  • Security: Protecting against fraud, unauthorized access, and harmful behavior
  • Communication: Providing service updates, security alerts, and support
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting regulatory requirements and responding to valid legal requests

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. Sharing only occurs in specific, limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting, payment processing, and analytics partners
  • Public Profiles: Usernames and rankings visible in leaderboards or social lobbies
  • Legal Necessity: When required to comply with law or protect rights and safety
  • Business Transitions: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

6. Your Privacy Rights & Control

Regardless of your location, we provide tools to manage your privacy:

  • Access & Portatiblity: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information directly via your profile
  • Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): Request the permanent deletion of your account and data
  • Processing Restriction: Opt-out of certain data uses, such as targeted marketing
  • Revocation of Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Privacy Team at connectboygames@gmail.com.

7. International Data Protection

We comply with major international frameworks, including GDPR (EU/UK), CCPA/CPRA (California), and LGPD (Brazil). We use Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) to ensure data remains protected during international transfers.

8. Children's Privacy

Protecting children is paramount. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. For users between 13 and 18, we recommend parental supervision and may require verifiable consent for certain features.
