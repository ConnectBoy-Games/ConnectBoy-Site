Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Connect Boy ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile applications and website (the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Registration: Username, email address, date of birth, and password
  • Payment Information: Credit card details, billing address (processed through secure third-party payment providers)
  • Profile Information: Avatar, bio, display name, and profile picture
  • Communication: Messages sent through in-game chat, support tickets, and customer service inquiries
  • Feedback: Surveys, feedback forms, and user reviews

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, mobile network information
  • Usage Data: Games played, gameplay statistics, achievements, time spent in games, features used
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address (optional GPS with your consent)
  • Cookies and Tracking: IP address, browser type, referring URL, pages visited
  • Analytics: App crashes, performance metrics, user interactions

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Social media platforms (if you connect your account)
  • Payment processors and fraud prevention services
  • Analytics and advertising partners
  • Other players (usernames and in-game interactions)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and improve the Service and our games
  • Account Management: To create and manage your account, process transactions, and provide customer support
  • Multiplayer Functionality: To enable multiplayer features, matchmaking, and cross-player interactions
  • Fraud Prevention: To detect and prevent cheating, unauthorized access, and fraudulent transactions
  • Personalization: To customize your gaming experience and provide relevant content recommendations
  • Marketing: To send promotional emails, announcements about new games, and special offers (with your consent)
  • Analytics: To analyze gameplay patterns, user engagement, and improve game balance
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with laws, regulations, and respond to legal requests
  • Safety and Security: To monitor for harmful, abusive, or illegal behavior

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Multiplayer Game Data

In our multiplayer games, other players will see:

  • Your username and avatar
  • Your in-game statistics, achievements, and leaderboard rankings
  • Messages you send in in-game chat
  • Clan or team membership (if applicable)

Your private information (email, real name, payment data) is never shared with other players.

4.2 Service Providers

We share information with trusted third parties who assist us:

  • Cloud hosting and data storage providers
  • Payment processors and payment gateways
  • Analytics and crash reporting services
  • Customer support platforms
  • Anti-cheat and fraud prevention services

These providers are contractually obligated to use your information solely for the purposes we specify.

4.3 Advertising Partners

We may share anonymized and aggregated data with advertising partners to deliver targeted ads. We do not share personally identifiable information with advertisers without your explicit consent.

4.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, legal process, or government request. We will notify you of such requests when legally permissible.

4.5 Business Transfers

If Connect Boy is acquired, merged, or sells its assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change.

5. Data Security

Connect Boy implements comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest
  • Secure password hashing and salting
  • Multi-factor authentication (optional)
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Restricted access to personal information (need-to-know basis)
  • Firewalls and intrusion detection systems

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the Service and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy.

  • Active Accounts: Information retained while your account is active
  • Account Deletion: Account data is deleted within 30 days of account closure, except where required by law
  • Backup Data: Backup copies may be retained for up to 90 days for disaster recovery purposes
  • Legal Hold: Data may be retained longer if required for legal disputes or investigations

7. Your Privacy Rights

7.1 Access and Portability

You have the right to access the personal information we hold about you and request a copy in a portable format.

7.2 Correction

You can update or correct your account information through your profile settings at any time.

7.3 Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data. We will delete your information within 30 days unless legally required to retain it.

7.4 Opt-Out

You can opt out of promotional emails by clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails or adjusting notification settings in your account preferences.

7.5 Do Not Track

Some browsers include a Do Not Track feature. Our Service does not currently respond to DNT signals.

7.6 GDPR Rights (EU Residents)

If you are located in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to be informed
  • Right of access
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@connectboy.com.

7.7 CCPA Rights (California Residents)

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, used, shared, or sold; the right to delete personal information; and the right to opt-out of the sale of personal information.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your login credentials and preferences
  • Track your gameplay and usage patterns
  • Deliver personalized content and advertisements
  • Analyze Service performance and user behavior

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may limit your ability to use certain features of the Service.

9. Third-Party Links and Services

The Service may contain links to third-party websites and services. Connect Boy is not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any information.

10. Children's Privacy

Connect Boy does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete such information and terminate the child's account.

For children between 13 and 18 years old, we require verifiable parental consent. Parents can contact us to review, modify, or delete their child's information.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Connect Boy may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Your continued use of the Service after such modifications constitute your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

12. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than where you reside. These countries may have data protection laws different from your home country. By using Connect Boy, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence, which may not have the same data protection laws.

We will take appropriate safeguards, including standard contractual clauses, to protect your information during international transfers.
